About JUST

Jamhuriya University of Science and Technology (JUST) is private institution and was officially established in Mogadishu, Somalia, in 2011 by a group of Somali scholars and Intellectuals to fill the existing vacuum (in the quality) in the higher education system in Somalia by providing higher education of international standards.Our academic staff is not only some of the smartest but passionate about providing students with up to date curriculums and text books and producing experienced, engaged successful graduates.JUST has an inviting environment with a variety of quality career-oriented under graduate programs to choose from, it further enhances the learning experiences with access to Technology infrastructure and very friendly and supporting educational staff.

Academics

Undergraduate

At JUST, we recognize that undergraduate education is not just about acquiring knowledge, but also about developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills. That's why our programs go beyond traditional classroom learning to offer experiential and hands-on opportunities such as internships, research projects, and community service. We also provide academic advising and support services to help students navigate their academic journey and make the most of their time at JUST. With a focus on academic excellence, innovation, and leadership, our undergraduate programs prepare our students for success in their careers and in life.

Postgraduate

The center for graduate studies (JCGS) of Jamhuriya University of Science & Technology is a postgraduate and higher learning center established by JUST to provide quality postgraduate research-oriented programs that further contribute to the community. JCGS strives to be at the forefront of national graduate and research advancements, founded on global standards and integration of research knowledge, and aspires to cultivate an environment that promotes both intellectual and social exchange among the students and faculty members with the ultimate goal of producing research of the highest quality, thus significantly contributing to the global knowledge creation and dissemination. We are very happy to provide you with JUST, center for graduate programs in areas, MBA Specializations, Hospital Management, Research & Statistics, Engineering, Computer field studies, Public Health, and Postgraduate Diplomas. We offer some affiliated programs with MAHSA University in Malaysia. Overall, these graduate programs offer students with advanced skills and knowledge in their chosen fields, allowing them to become leaders in their professions and make valuable contributions to their organizations and society as a whole. Postgraduate Diplomas are specialized graduate-level programs designed to run students with advanced knowledge and skills in a particular field. They are typically shorter than traditional graduate programs and can be completed in less time. Postgraduate Diplomas vary depending on the specific program.

Admission Requirements

Education

  • Must have Completed Secondary School Certificate accredited by the Ministry of Higher Education

Certificate

  • Should bring the original Secondary School Leaving Certificate.
  • Include two (2) photocopies of the certificate.

Photos

  • Should bring four (4) passport-size photos.
  • Background must be white.

Research

The objective of the Research Conference on Engineering, Science, and Technology Collaboration, in partnership with Jamhuriya University of Science and Technology, SIMAD University, and ZAMZAM University, is to establish a vibrant platform that fosters cross-institutional cooperation, facilitates the exchange of cutting-edge research, promotes multidisciplinary engagement, empowers emerging scholars, bridges the gap between academia and industry, cultivates global perspectives, enhances research impact through publication, and inspires collaborative research initiatives. By bringing together academia, researchers, industry professionals, and students, the conference aims to catalyze innovation, address complex challenges, and contribute to the advancement of engineering, science, and technology on both regional and global scales.
